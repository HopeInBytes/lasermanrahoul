Laserman Rahoul is a world-renowned performer specializing in laser manipulation and light illusions. His performances captivate audiences worldwide.

Rahoul, known as "The Laserman Rahoul" for his captivating performances, has trained TV stars, assisted top choreographers, and performed in numerous shows. He has worked with legends like Puneet Pathak, Vaibhavi Merchant, Remo D’Souza, and Geeta Kapoor. In August 2016, he launched Rhythmic Squad Dance Studio (RSDS), announcing it live on Red FM 93.5.
